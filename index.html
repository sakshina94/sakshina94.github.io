Workforce Development

Workforce Pathways Navigator

An interactive tool that helps people facing employment barriers — justice involvement, limited education, immigration status, transportation challenges — discover realistic career pathways in AI-resilient occupations. A 6-step assessment wizard evaluates eligibility across 24+ skilled trades, healthcare, and high-demand occupations, accounting for conviction history, state licensing rules, drug screening, physical limitations, and financial constraints. Results include personalized remediation steps, state-specific policy guidance, funding sources (WIOA, Pell, apprenticeships), and AI automation risk scores.
